Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: July 3, 2026 JivaOne v1.0 SMD_Ai

💰 Refund Policy Overview

JivaOne Pro recharges are non-recurring and instantly grant digital access to advanced local AI utility capabilities. Because features are provisioned immediately upon purchase, all payments are non-refundable. However, in cases of payment failures or processing errors where your account is not upgraded, we resolve and issue refunds or upgrades within 5 to 7 business days.

1 Subscription Cancellation

Pro Plans for JivaOne operate on a prepaid, non-recurring recharge basis (e.g., a 30-day Pro recharge or a 365-day Pro recharge).

  • No Auto-Renewal: Your credit card, debit card, or UPI account will not be automatically debited at the end of your plan period.
  • No Cancellation Fee: You do not need to take any action to cancel your subscription. Once your recharge period (30 or 365 days) expires, your account will automatically downgrade to the Free Tier.
  • No Part-Period Refunds: If you decide to stop using the application before the end of your paid plan, you will not receive a refund for the remaining days of your active plan. Your access will remain active until the end of the current period.

2 Refund Terms & Conditions

Since JivaOne Pro is a digital software utility that is activated immediately upon successful transaction completion, all purchases are final.

General Policy: We do not offer cash, UPI, or bank refunds, credits, or exchanges for change-of-mind, unused days, or if you choose to uninstall the application.

3 Failed Transactions & Billing Issues

In rare circumstances, a payment transaction may fail or experience delays due to network errors.

3.1 Double Debits / Failed Provisioning

If your money is deducted from your bank account or wallet, but your JivaOne client app fails to upgrade to the Pro plan (due to connection loss or sync failure):

  1. Check if the payment was actually successful by looking for a receipt from Razorpay in your email inbox.
  2. If you were debited but did not receive a confirmation receipt, the amount is usually auto-refunded by your bank or Razorpay within 2-3 business days.
  3. If you did receive a Razorpay receipt but the JivaOne client continues to show the Free Tier, contact our support immediately.

3.2 How to Claim a Refund/Activation

To claim an activation or a refund for a failed provisioning issue, please email us at jiva.assistant@gmail.com with the following details:

  • Your JivaOne account email address.
  • Razorpay Order ID or Payment ID (e.g., pay_XYZ...).
  • A screenshot of the payment receipt or transaction confirmation from your bank/wallet.

Once verified, we will either manually activate your Pro plan subscription or issue a full refund to the original source of payment within 5 to 7 business days.

4 Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any issues related to payment or provisioning errors before filing a chargeback or payment dispute with your bank or payment gateway. Filing a dispute increases processing times, whereas we can directly upgrade your account or issue a refund much faster if you email us.

5 Policy Revisions

We reserve the right to alter, modify, or update this Refund & Cancellation Policy at any time. Any changes will become effective immediately upon posting on this website. Active subscriptions will remain subject to the policy that was in force at the exact time of the transaction.
